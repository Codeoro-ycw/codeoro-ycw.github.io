Our Story

Luna Threads was born from a desire to merge fashion with function. We believe clothing should be clean, purposeful, and personal. From our design studio in Maryland, we craft collections that reflect bold simplicity and sustainable values.

Mission & Values

  • Ethical sourcing and fair labor practices
  • Inclusive sizing and accessible fashion
  • Timeless, sustainable design over fast fashion
