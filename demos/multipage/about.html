About Threadly

Founded in 2022, Threadly is a modern clothing brand driven by sustainability and bold style. We design streetwear that speaks — to youth, creatives, and anyone with something to say.

Based in Maryland, we focus on ethical sourcing and local production. Every hoodie, tee, and jacket is crafted with care, combining comfort with cutting-edge design.

From art collabs to seasonal collections, we’re more than clothes — we’re a culture.
